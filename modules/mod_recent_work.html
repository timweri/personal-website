Facelink

Hack the North 2018

Built with: HTML5, CSS3, JQuery, JavaScript, Firebase, Kairos, Bootstrap

Description: A facial recognition web app that uses Kairos library to securely links facial signatures to students' resumes. Students just need to register an account and upload their resumes in order to easily submit their resumes at recruiters' booths at job fairs by scanning their faces.
